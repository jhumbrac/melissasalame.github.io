Grants

B*CURED's primary goal is to fund innovative brain cancer research to end brain cancer.

Our funding for the following grants bring us closer to finding a cure.

We invite clinical doctors and research scientists whose principal focus is brain cancer to apply for our grants. Clinical and translational projects with significant clinical promise will be considered.

Since it's establishment in 2008, B*CURED has funded 21 programs at prominent US hospitals and universities.

For further information, please contact us at research@bcured.org.
